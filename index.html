About This App

We created the Masked Buffalo to ensure that our Buffalo community knows where supplies can be found during this difficult time. Buffalo is the city of Good Neighbors for a reason, and together we will get through this pandemic stronger than ever!

How it Works

Pharmacists in the Buffalo area will provide up-to-date information on their supplies, allowing you to be sure you can get what you need.
Pharmacists can click here to sign up
Click the Find Supplies tab to find out where to find what you need!
